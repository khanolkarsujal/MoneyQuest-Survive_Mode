# 🧭 MoneyQuest – Survive Mode

MoneyQuest is a **Python Streamlit application** designed to help students manage their spending and survive the last days of the month without running out of money.

The system calculates a **Safe Daily Spending Limit** based on your current cash and upcoming bills.

If you stay under your daily limit, you can avoid the **end-of-month money crisis**.

---

# 🌐 Live Demo

Try the app here:

https://moneyquest-survive-mode.streamlit.app/

---

# 🎯 Problem

Many students face a **financial crunch during the last week of the month** because they:

* Don't track daily spending
* Ignore small expenses
* Have no clear daily spending limit

MoneyQuest helps prevent this by acting like a **financial survival compass**.

---

# 💡 Solution

MoneyQuest helps users:

* Set a **budget**
* Track **daily expenses**
* Monitor **remaining balance**
* Calculate a **safe daily spending limit**
* Predict **how long money will last**

# 🚀 How It Works

### Step 1 — Setup Budget

Enter:

* Current cash
* Upcoming bills (rent, mess, etc.)

This forms your **monthly spending plan**.

---

### Step 2 — Log Expenses

Every time you spend money (chai, food, bus, etc.), log the expense.

This keeps your spending record updated.

---

### Step 3 — Check Dashboard

The dashboard shows:

* Remaining cash
* Safe daily spending limit
* Survival time
* Spending status

Green Zone → Safe spending
Red Zone → Overspending
